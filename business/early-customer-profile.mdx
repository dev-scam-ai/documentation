## Key Characteristics

- **Size**: 50-100 employees
- **Urgency**: Ready to try and pay immediately
- **Access**: Through trusted networks (advisors, SkyDecks, existing connections)
- **Pain Point**: Either need to enhance existing security testing or establish it for the first time
- **Industry Focus**: Information-sensitive sectors, particularly healthcare
